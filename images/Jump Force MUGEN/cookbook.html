🥗 Vegetarian Recipes

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup cream

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter and sauté onions.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook.
  3. Add paneer cubes and cream.
  4. Simmer and serve hot.

Veg Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. Fry veggies with spices.
  3. Mix with rice and serve.

Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 cups spinach puree
  • 1 onion, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Fry onions and garlic in butter.
  2. Add spinach puree and cook.
  3. Add paneer cubes and simmer.
  4. Serve hot with roti.

🍗 Non-Vegetarian Recipes

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed

Instructions:

  1. Fry onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and chicken.
  3. Cook until tender.
  4. Serve hot with rice.

Fish Fry

Ingredients:

  • 4 fish fillets
  • Spices and lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Marinate fish with spices.
  2. Fry until golden brown.
  3. Serve with lemon wedges.
